CITY OF ANGELS is an other emotional drama that i fell in love with. But this time, it is a story about an angel named Seth who was sent to eath in order to help and look for humains. While he was doing his duty, he met a beautiful heart surgen named Maggie . As the time pass by, the angel fell in love with Maggie. But beacause he was an angel, he had to end his feelings otherwise he looses all his powers and become human.

Eventually, he chosed the last option and became mortal. But at the end, he realised that he lost everything : his powers and the love of his life Maggie. It's a sad story but it's worth watching.
